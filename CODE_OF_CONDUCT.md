# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and kindness toward others
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
publishing others' private information without permission, and other conduct which
could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to
the project maintainers via the private security/contact channel described in
[`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly.
